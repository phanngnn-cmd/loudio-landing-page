## Error Prevention

- **Anti-pattern**: Quantity over quality. 10 qualified leads > 100 random venues.
- **Anti-pattern**: Ignoring reviews. Music mentions in reviews = gold.
- **Anti-pattern**: Only using Google Maps. Instagram shows true vibe.
- **Anti-pattern**: No competitive intel. Know what you're replacing.
- **Anti-pattern**: Guessing decision-maker. Verify before outreach.

## Research Techniques

**Instagram Deep Dive**:

1. Search location tags for venue
2. Check venue's tagged photos (customer perspective)
3. Review staff accounts for insider info
4. Note event frequency and types

**Review Mining**:

1. Filter for "music" keyword in Google/Foody reviews
2. Look for complaints ("too loud", "boring playlist")
3. Look for praise ("great vibe", "love the music")

**Timing Intelligence**:

- Best months: March-May (post-Tet), Sept-Nov (pre-holiday)
- Best outreach: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM
- Avoid: Monday (busy), Friday (prep for weekend)

## First Principles for Venue Scouting

**Work Backwards from Ideal Customer**:

- Who is the PERFECT Loudio venue? High foot traffic, music-centric, GenZ audience, owner accessible.
- Find more venues like your best customers.

**Signals Over Assumptions**:

- Don't assume a bar needs music. Find EVIDENCE of music importance.
- Reviews mentioning music = validated signal.

**Quality of Intel = Quality of Sale**:

- A well-researched venue brief saves hours in sales conversations.
- Invest 15 minutes in research to save 2 hours in objections.
